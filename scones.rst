=================
Brentwood Squares
=================

* 6oz plain chocolate
* 4oz cherries
* 4oz sultanas
* 4oz soft margarine
* 6oz caster sugar
* 2 eggs
* 8oz coconut (dessicated)

1. Grease and line tin approx 9 x 13inches
2. Melt choc and spread over greaseproof / parchment. Leave in fridge till hard
3. Rinse and chop cherries, chop sultanas.
4. Cream margarine and sugar till fluffy.
5. Beat eggs and add gradually, beating well.
6. Add cherries, sultanas and coconut and mix well.
7. Spread evenly over chocolate.
8. Bake centre of oven 30-35 minutes till golden brown. Leave in tin till
   completely cold then cut into squares
